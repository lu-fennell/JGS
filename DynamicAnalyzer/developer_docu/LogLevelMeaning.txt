In Order to communicate the LocLevel and there meanings.

SEVERE:
	The absolute highest and worst thinkable Scenario,
	e.g. catching (unexpected) Exceptions.

WARNING:
	Some case, that may cause throwing an exception
	or something else, the may be called warning.

INFO:
	Messages, that tent to inform the User of something.
	Here shall not be any Debug Messages.

CONFIG:
	Messages, that are used for Configuration, this may be 
	the highest debugging level.

FINE:
	Messages, that are lower configurations and higher debug messages.

FINER:
	The normal Debug Level, where The Enter and Exit Logs are present.

FINEST:
	Debug Messages, that are used to debug in very detail.
